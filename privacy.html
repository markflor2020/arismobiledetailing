Privacy Policy

Plain English, and specific to what this website actually does. Last updated July 16, 2026.

The short version

  • This site sets no cookies and uses no analytics or advertising trackers.
  • The only personal information we collect is what you type into our quote form (or tell us when you call/text).
  • We use it to get back to you with a quote. We do not sell or share it for advertising.

Who we are

Ari's Mobile Detailing ("we," "us," "our") is a mobile auto detailing business serving Omaha, Nebraska and the surrounding metro. This policy covers the website arismobiledetailing.com.

Questions, or want your information deleted? Call or text (402) 515-9157, or email arismobiledetailing@gmail.com.

What we collect

1. Information you give us

When you submit the quote form, we receive only what you enter:

  • Your name
  • Your phone number
  • Your vehicle
  • The service you're interested in
  • Any details you choose to add

Submitting the form is entirely voluntary. If you call or text us instead, we'll have your phone number and whatever you tell us.

2. Information collected automatically

Our website host, Cloudflare, processes standard technical information needed to deliver the site and protect it from abuse — such as your IP address, browser type, and which pages were requested. We do not use this to build profiles or identify individual visitors.

What we don't do

  • No cookies. This site does not set cookies.
  • No analytics. There is no Google Analytics or any other analytics tool on this site.
  • No advertising trackers. No Meta/Facebook Pixel, no Google Ads tags, no retargeting pixels.
  • No third-party fonts or embeds. The fonts on this site are served from our own server, so no outside font provider receives your IP address. There are no embedded maps, videos, or social widgets.
  • No selling or sharing. We do not sell your personal information, and we do not share it for cross-context behavioral advertising.

How we use your information

Only to respond to you and do the work: to send you a quote, schedule your detail, and follow up about your service. That's it. We don't add you to marketing lists without you asking.

Who we share it with

We don't sell your information. We do rely on a few service providers to run the site:

  • Web3Forms — processes quote-form submissions and delivers them to our email inbox. Your form contents pass through them. (their privacy policy)
  • Cloudflare — hosts and delivers this website and processes request data as described above. (their privacy policy)
  • Our email provider — receives and stores quote requests so we can reply.

We may also disclose information if required by law.

Links to Instagram and Facebook

The social icons on this site are ordinary links — we do not embed Instagram or Facebook tracking code. If you click through, those companies' own privacy policies apply to what they collect on their platforms.

How long we keep it

Quote requests stay in our email so we can serve you and keep a record of the work. If you'd like your information deleted, just ask and we'll remove it.

Your choices and rights

You can ask us to provide a copy of, correct, or delete the personal information you've given us. Email arismobiledetailing@gmail.com or call/text (402) 515-9157 and we'll take care of it within a reasonable time. We won't treat you differently for asking.

Depending on where you live, you may have additional rights under state privacy laws. Because we don't sell your information or share it for targeted advertising, there is no advertising opt-out to offer.

Children

This site isn't directed at children under 13, and we don't knowingly collect their personal information.

Changes to this policy

If we change how we handle information — for example, if we ever add analytics or advertising tools — we'll update this page and the "last updated" date above.
